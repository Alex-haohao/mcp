# Identity Notes

- Stable silhouette: chibi StackChan-ready bust with oversized round head, compact small body, twin-tail side hair volume, left-side short bob mass, right-side long blue ponytail mass, small mitten-like hands, black oversized coat.
- Palette: pale skin, bright blue eyes, black-to-deep-navy bangs, saturated royal-blue hair streaks, light powder-blue long hair, charcoal black coat, white fluffy hair puffs, muted gray bow/scarf accents, small pink cheek accents.
- Line quality: hatch-pet-inspired pixel-art-adjacent mascot; chunky dark outline, limited palette, flat cel shading, visible stepped edges, crisp small-screen readability, no painterly anti-noise.
- Material/finish: digital pixel sprite, clean hard-edged chroma-key extraction, no soft glows or shadows.
- Eye language: large glossy blue anime eyes simplified into pixel-art shapes; expressive eyelids and brows allowed, but keep same eye color, size family, and sparkle placement.
- Mouth language: small chibi mouth shapes; neutral tiny smile, happy open smile, angry tight shape, sad downturned shape, doubt asymmetry, sleepy small relaxed mouth. Speech frames progress closed to wide open.
- Permanent accessories: white fluffy puffs near both upper side hair positions, dark oversized coat, gray dotted bow/scarf area. Keep accessories as body/base identity, not separate floating decorators.
- Forbidden drift: no realistic rendering, no full-body tall human proportions, no extra scenery, no text, no logos, no white/black background, no losing twin tails, no changing blue-black hair split, no removing fluffy white hair puffs, no changing coat into another outfit, no detached effects in eye/mouth strips.
