# Art Direction

- Chosen candidate: neutral-style-2.
- Why it looks good: strong cute idol identity, clear face, balanced chibi proportions, and enough detail without overwhelming the small screen.
- Pixel/sprite qualities to preserve: compact silhouette, dark outline, limited palette, flat cel shading, hard opaque edges, no soft painterly background effects.
- Color harmony: blue/cyan hair and eyes against black sleeves, with pink blush as the main warm accent.
- Readability at 320x240: face, twin tails, black sleeves, and ribbon must remain readable.
- Expression principles: emotion changes should primarily use eyes, eyelids, eyebrows, mouth, and slight face attitude; body shape remains stable.
- Repair priorities: preserve identity first, then expression readability, then pixel/sprite cleanliness, then decorator polish.
